About CINEMATIC

Founded in 2002 by Laura Galindo and Vinaya Seshan, CINEMATIC takes cinema experience to the next level. In the past 19 years, Cinematic has been offering the best movie experiences in France and has expanded to over 50 Box Offices around the country! With over 100,000 members and over 60,000 screenings so far, CINEMATIC is a one ticket stop for endless memories.
